About me

I am a Research Scientist at NVIDIA's Deep Imagination Research group. I am broadly interested in 3D reconstruction, generation, robotics, and embodied systems, all of which are connected to the concept of spatial intelligence. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Johns Hopkins University on visual servoing for surgical robotics, advised by Dr. Russell H Taylor and co-advised by Dr. Mathias Unberath. I received my Bachelor degree from University of British Columbia, advised by Dr. Tim Salcudean. I have been fortunate to collaborate with many brilliant minds at NVIDIA, Meta Reality Labs, Intuitive, UBC and JHU.
