About me

I am a Computer Science PhD student with Prof. Russell H Taylor and Prof. Mathias Unberath at Johns Hopkins University. My research interests include surgical robotics, vision navigation and 3D reconstruction. I have been fortunate to collaborate with many brilliant minds at JHU, Nvidia, Reality Labs and Intuitive. I am also a videographer, a mental health promoter, and a dog lover.

I am looking for a computer vision position in industry starting Summer/Fall 2023.
